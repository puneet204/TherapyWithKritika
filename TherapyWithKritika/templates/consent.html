I understand that therapy involves professional services to assist with personal, emotional, psychological, and/or relationship concerns.

Goals of Therapy

  • Understanding and managing emotions
  • Improving communication skills
  • Resolving conflicts
  • Developing coping strategies
  • Enhancing personal growth and self-awareness
  • Therapy is not intended for seeking advice

Confidentiality

All information shared during therapy is confidential except where required by law.

  • Risk of harm to self or others
  • Suspected abuse or neglect
  • Court order or subpoena
  • Professional consultation within ethical guidelines

Benefits and Risks

Therapy may improve relationships, confidence, and coping skills. However, it may also involve discussing difficult emotions.

Scheduling and Communication

Clients are expected to attend scheduled sessions and inform in advance for cancellations or rescheduling. Communication outside sessions is limited to emergencies.

Cancellation Policy

  • Confirm appointments at least 24 hours in advance
  • Provide 24-hour notice for cancellation/rescheduling
  • Late cancellations may incur a fee
  • No-shows will be charged the full session fee
  • Exceptions may apply for emergencies
  • Frequent cancellations may require pre-payment
  • Outstanding fees must be cleared before next session

Fees and Payment

Clients agree to follow the fee structure and make timely payments.

Termination of Therapy

Therapy is voluntary. Either party may terminate based on professional or ethical considerations.
