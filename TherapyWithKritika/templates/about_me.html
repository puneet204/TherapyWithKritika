About Me

Hi, I’m Kritika Zutshi, a clinical psychologist based in India, offering online therapy for individuals seeking clarity, healing, and growth.

I believe therapy is not about fixing you, but about understanding you — your experiences, your emotions, and the unique story that has shaped who you are.

My work focuses on helping you explore your inner world, make sense of your thoughts and feelings, and build a more grounded, compassionate relationship with yourself.

I aim to create a space that feels safe, supportive, and non-judgmental — where you can show up as you are and have real, meaningful conversations.
