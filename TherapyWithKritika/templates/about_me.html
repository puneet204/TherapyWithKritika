About Kritika

A practice built on connection, not just technique

I'm Kritika Zutshi, a licensed clinical psychologist based in Pune.
I hold an M.Phil. in Clinical Psychology from Central Institute of Psychiatry, Ranchi (CIP).
For over six years I've worked with adults navigating anxiety, depression, trauma, grief, and the quieter crises that don't always have names.

My approach draws from cognitive-behavioral therapy, internal family systems, and acceptance-based frameworks — but I believe the foundation of any real therapeutic change is in the relationship between the client and therapist.
I work best with people who are ready to be honest, even when it's uncomfortable.
