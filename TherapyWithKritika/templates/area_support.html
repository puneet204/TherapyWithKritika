Services

How I can help you

Every person arrives with a different story. I offer a focused range of evidence-based services designed to address the issues most common in adult mental health — approached with curiosity rather than formula.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one sessions exploring the roots of your patterns and building new ways of responding. Sessions are 60 minutes.

Anxiety & Depression

Targeted CBT and ACT-based work to interrupt anxious cycles, reduce avoidance, and rebuild a life that feels livable again.

Trauma & PTSD

Trauma-focused therapy to process difficult experiences at your own pace, reducing their power over your present life.

Life Transitions

Support through divorce, career change, loss, relocation, retirement, or any passage that has left you uncertain of who you are now.

Grief & Loss

A space to mourn what has been lost — a person, a relationship, a version of yourself — without timeline or expectation.

Telehealth Sessions

Secure video sessions for clients who prefer to meet from home.

My Approach

Therapy that fits your life, not a protocol

I don't believe in one-size-fits-all treatment. Every session begins with listening, and every plan is built together. You bring the expertise on yourself — I bring the clinical knowledge and the structure that makes change possible.

01
Free 15-minute consultation

We meet briefly — no charge, no commitment — to see if working together makes sense. You can ask anything. This will not be a therapy session but a space to understand the structure and flow of the sessions.

02
Comprehensive intake assessment

A thorough first session to understand your history, your goals, and what has and hasn't worked before.

03
Tailored treatment plan

We build a plan together — drawing on appropriate therapeutic approaches based on what fits your needs and preferences.

04
Ongoing work & review

Regular check-ins on progress. The plan adapts as you do. Sessions are weekly, bi-weekly or fortnightly depending on your goals and needs.
